A Community Modding Site

Kerbal Space Program has long been plagued by poor modding sites. There have been sites that are just plain bad, and sites that look suspiciously like money exchanged hands to get them in place. Squad has endorsed these sites all the same, and so we thought it was about time for the community to step in and run our own modding site. Kerbal Stuff is that site, and aims to fix the problems with the old and bring the community back into the loop.

Kerbal Stuff is great for

  • For Players
  • For Modders

Emails when mods update

Easily keep track of your favorite mods. Don't bother checking the forums every 5 minutes, just sit back and we'll email you when the time comes.

Easy to use search

Unlike some previous attempts, Kerbal Stuff puts some effort into the search to make sure you find what you're looking for on the first try.

Changelogs and old versions

Got a nostalgic itch to scratch? Kerbal Stuff keeps around old versions of mods and includes the last version of KSP they worked with.

Find new and cool mods

You already know the mods everyone's using. Kerbal Stuff features only new and interesting mods on the front page. Established mods don't need the exposure.

Keep track of your mods

We'll give you a history of downloads and we'll give it to you with nice pretty graphs. You can also export the raw data if you want.

Update in moments

No nonsense updates. Give us a version number, a zip file, and optionally a changelog, and your followers get an email with the latest version.

Easy to find the old, exposure for the new

Got a popular mod? It'll be the first in the search results. Got a brand-new mod? We'll make sure you get the exposure you need.

Open source

Wouldn't it be great if you could help make your modding site even better? You can! Kerbal Stuff is open source.
