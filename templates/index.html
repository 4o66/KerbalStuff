Welcome to Kerbal Stuff

You've found Kerbal Stuff, a simple website for Kerbal Space Program mods. To upload some mods, create an account. To find some new mods, start looking around. If you'd like, we have a mod manager that you can install to make your life easier. It's not required, though. You'll get no wait times and won't get shafted over features if you choose not to install anything - and there's no "premium" plan. Also, Kerbal Stuff is open source.
